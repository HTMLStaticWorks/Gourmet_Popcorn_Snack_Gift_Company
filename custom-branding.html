Corporate Custom Branding Guidelines

Download printing specifications and review customization assets to align our gift packaging with your brand book.

1. Custom Printed Metal Lids

We utilize state-of-the-art flatbed UV printing technology to cure ink directly onto silver, gold, or white metal lids. The result is a vibrant, durable, scratch-resistant logo finish that serves as a beautiful corporate keepsake long after the snacks are gone.

File Format Requirements

We accept vector formats (SVG, EPS, PDF) with outlined fonts, or high-resolution raster files (minimum 300 DPI PSD or transparent PNG) scaled to full tin dimensions.

2. Satin Ribbons & Custom Sleeves

In addition to custom lid prints, we offer custom-dyed satin ribbons to match Pantone colors for orders exceeding 200 units. For smaller collections, select from our catalog of 12 classic satin ribbon styles.

Packaging Card Sleeves

Wrap any standard popcorn box with a fully-printed cardboard card sleeve. Perfect for incorporating comprehensive brand storylines or seasonal graphic details.
